Who Is BUYCO For

Built for the
Independent Retailer.

BUYCO is not your average buying group. It's a bold new movement designed for ambitious, owner-operated hardware and building supply stores across Southern Africa.

Born out of the trusted reputation of The Building Company, the powerhouse behind BUCO and Timbercity, BUYCO brings corporate-scale buying power to the table — with streamlined processes and procedures.

These independent Building Material Merchants are entrepreneurial, community-rooted store owners who want the freedom to grow their business on their own terms, without sacrificing margin, or identity.
