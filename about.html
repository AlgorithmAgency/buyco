Who We Are

Born From a
Trusted Legacy.

BUYCO is The Building Company's Collective Buying Group — a bold new model that bridges the gap between independent retail and structured national procurement across Southern Africa.

Born out of the trusted reputation of The Building Company, the powerhouse behind BUCO and Timbercity, BUYCO brings corporate-scale buying power directly to owner-managed stores — with streamlined processes and full commercial support.

We are a progressive ecosystem designed to bring together forward-thinking suppliers and independent retailers under one profitable, transparent platform.

How We Operate

A Different Kind of
Buying Group.

BUYCO is not a passive procurement portal. We are an active commercial partner built on three operating principles.

01
⚡

Performance-Led
